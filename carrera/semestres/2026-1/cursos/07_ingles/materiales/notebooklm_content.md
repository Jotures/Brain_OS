## 📚 LIBRO 1: BASIC (Nivel A1)

### Unidad 1-4: Fundamentos
- Verb TO BE (am, is, are) - Afirmativo, negativo, interrogativo
- Subject Pronouns (I, you, he, she, it, we, they)
- Possessive Adjectives (my, your, his, her, its, our, their)
- Greetings and Introductions

### Unidad 5-8: Acciones Básicas
- Present Simple Tense - Uso y conjugación
- Adverbs of Frequency (always, usually, sometimes, never)
- Question Words (what, where, when, who, why, how)
- Daily Routines vocabulary

### Unidad 9-12: Descripciones
- Demonstrative Pronouns (this, that, these, those)
- There is / There are
- Prepositions of Place (in, on, at, under, behind)
- Describing places and objects

### Unidad 13-16: Acciones en Progreso
- Present Continuous Tense (am/is/are + -ing)
- Difference: Present Simple vs Present Continuous
- Time expressions for each tense
- Common -ing spelling rules

---

## 📚 LIBRO 2: HIGH BASIC (Nivel A1-A2)

### Unidad 1-4: Pasado
- Past Simple: Verb TO BE (was/were)
- Past Simple: Regular Verbs (-ed endings)
- Past Simple: Irregular Verbs (went, saw, had, etc.)
- Time expressions for past (yesterday, last week, ago)

### Unidad 5-8: Contables e Incontables
- Countable vs Uncountable Nouns
- Quantifiers: some, any, much, many, a lot of
- How much / How many questions
- Food and drink vocabulary

### Unidad 9-12: Comparaciones
- Comparative Adjectives (bigger, more expensive)
- Superlative Adjectives (the biggest, the most expensive)
- Irregular comparatives (good-better-best)
- Comparing people, places, and things

### Unidad 13-16: Futuro y Modales
- WILL for predictions and spontaneous decisions
- BE GOING TO for plans and intentions
- Modal Verbs: can, could, may, might, must, should
- Expressing ability, possibility, and obligation

---

## 📚 LIBRO 3: PRE-INTERMEDIATE I (Nivel A2)

### Unidad 1-4: Tiempos Perfectos
- Present Perfect Simple (have/has + past participle)
- Since vs For
- Ever, never, already, yet, just
- Present Perfect vs Past Simple

### Unidad 5-8: Estructuras Avanzadas
- First Conditional (If + present, will + infinitive)
- Relative Clauses (who, which, that, where)
- Defining vs Non-defining relative clauses
- Complex sentence structures

### Unidad 9-12: Pasado Continuo
- Past Continuous (was/were + -ing)
- Past Continuous vs Past Simple
- When and While in past narratives
- Telling stories and anecdotes

### Unidad 13-16: Infinitivo y Gerundio
- Verbs followed by infinitive (want to, need to, decide to)
- Verbs followed by gerund (enjoy, finish, avoid + -ing)
- Verbs that take both (remember, stop, try)
- Common phrasal verbs
