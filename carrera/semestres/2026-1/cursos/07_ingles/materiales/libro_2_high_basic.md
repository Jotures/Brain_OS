## Scope & Sequence

| Unit | Título | Gramática Clave |
|------|--------|-----------------|
| 1 | I want to join the Chess Club | Modals: can/could/would + like/should |
| 2 | How many cousins do you have? | Comparative & Superlative Adjectives |
| 3 | I'm taking an art class next month | Present Progressive for Future, Question words |
| 4 | We went on vacation | Past Simple, "That sounds..." |
| 5 | Can I have a bottle of water? | Containers, Present Perfect Progressive |
| 6 | Where do you work? | Like + Gerund, Get to vs Have to |
| 7 | Do you need a hand? | Like/Want/Need, Modals, Polite expressions |
| 8 | You should make a reservation | Should vs Have to, Modals |
| 9 | How do I get there? | Directions, Prepositions of Location |
| 10 | I eat too much junk food | Determiners: much/many/enough, Used to |
| 11 | I got promoted! | Meanings of get, 1st & 2nd Conditionals |
| 12 | I don't feel well | Should for advice, Perfect Progressive |
| 13 | He's the best candidate | More/most for comparison, Job skills |
| 14 | Have you ever been to the zoo? | Perfect tenses, Never/ever/already/yet |
| 15 | I'm thinking about studying abroad | Thinking about + Gerund, Why/Because |
| 16 | I forgot to get some milk | Questions in the past, Be able to |
